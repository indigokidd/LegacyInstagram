I’ve known people over the years who’ve “broke through” and had their analytics jump because they kept grinding, and I’ve known people who’ve just factored in buying plays into a strategic way to reach more people.. So, been seeing a lot of AI “art” stuff around, and have been seeing both sides of the topic. Since we have two people in our band that perform visual art to varying degrees, we thought we would simply let you all decide.. the first is a picture is a picture of Dalton, Garrett, and Eli in San Francisco.. as you swipe right you will see an artificial reimagining of this picture prompted with phrases ranging from “young cowboys studying the sprawling frontier” to “three young handsome professional wrestlers leading a cat army”… Fun stuff.. Also, just a quick disclaimer.. artists, after playing around with this program, I don’t think AI is coming for your jobs.. it’s obviously still a hodgepodge of already existing work, and the only original aspect of AI art is prob the ability to put a huge disfigured cat arm growing out of your gut. Not to make an artistic statement, but because it doesn’t understand that cat arms don’t usually protrude out of a human stomach. That being said, I do think it’s up to people who appreciate original art to defend it anyway we can.. Unfortunately, AI art is so new that it’s hard to quite say what that would be, buttttttttt if there’s going to be a betrayal of art and culture it’s probably going to happen by the hand of unshackled capitalism and that is where we should be looking for this new method to be misused.. I know it seems like a random tangent, but I thought I would at least address this new medium in its complexity rather than fully embrace or reject it with the seemingly limited information that the typical “tech normie” like myself would have.. -Eli Let us know what you think in the comments! 👍
